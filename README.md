# Singapore Housing Market Insights

**By Mya Thet Mon**

---

## Overview

This project analyzes Singapore’s HDB housing market using datasets from data.gov.sg. It explores **price trends, affordability, sales vs rental patterns, and household distribution** across different flat types.

The goal is to uncover insights into housing demand and market behavior through data analysis and visualization.

---

## 📂 Datasets

* Price Range of HDB Flats Offered
* Number of Sold and Rented HDB Residential Units
* HDB Households by Flat Type

Source: [https://data.gov.sg](https://data.gov.sg)

---

## Tech Stack

* Python
* NumPy
* Matplotlib

---

## Key Insights

#### Price Trends

* Prices increase with flat size
* Maximum prices show **high volatility**
* Minimum prices remain **stable over time**

---

#### Affordability by Town

* Most affordable: **Sengkang**
* More expensive towns: **Jurong East, Hougang, Tengah**
* Mid-range: Bukit Batok, Jurong West, Punggol, Choa Chu Kang

---

#### Market Trends

* Sales fluctuate significantly across years
* Rentals remain **low and stable**

---

#### Sales vs Rental Distribution

* Sales dominated by **4-room flats (~50%)**
* Rentals dominated by **1-room & 2-room flats**

---

#### Household Distribution

* Most households live in:

  * 4-room flats (highest)
  * 3-room and 5-room flats
* Least: 1-room and 2-room flats

## Limitations

* Missing values treated as 0
* Limited time range
* No external economic factors included

## Conclusion

The HDB market shows:

* Stable minimum prices but volatile maximum prices
* Strong demand for 4-room flats in sales
* High rental demand for smaller flats

This project provides useful insights into **housing affordability and demand patterns in Singapore**.
